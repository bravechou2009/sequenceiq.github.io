Careers

Data scientist

Responsibilities

  • Work closely with the product engineering team to identify and answer important product questions
  • Comfort manipulating and analyzing complex, high-volume, high-dimensionality data from varying sources
  • Answer product questions by using appropriate statistical techniques on available data
  • Analyze and interpret the results of product experiments
  • Build and refine models for our customers

Requirements

  • M.S. or Ph.D. in a relevant technical field, or experience in a relevant role
  • Ability to communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise, and actionable manner
  • Knowledge of an analysis tool such as R, Matlab, Julia or SAS
  • A strong passion for empirical research and for answering hard questions with data
  • Strong oral and written English communication skills
  • Hadoop experience is desirable but not exclusive

Last but not the least, candidates should be excited to help build our YARN/Hadoop 2.x based ecosystem and to participate in building our company culture as expressed by our core values. If you are in search of an opportunity for a startup company that fosters innovation and creativity, while delivering cutting edge technologies, then submit your resume today.
