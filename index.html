Jobs

SequenceIQ is a ~10 employee, self-sustained startup which is entering a high growth phase. We are looking for exceptional software engineers to join our development team. Are you a JAVA developer interested in Big Data and have experienced with Hadoop or interested to learn? Are you interested to work with others in the open source community?

Full stack developer

Responsibilities

  • Software design and development with strong JAVA skills and understanding of distributed & large scale systems
  • Hands on technical role; contribute to all phases of the software development lifecycle, including the analysis, architecture, design, implementation, and test
  • Formulate and present architecture and design documents internally and to the open source community
  • Build a Hadoop 2.0/YARN based platform using Tez/Stinger initiative (for Hive and MR) and Hoya(for HBase)
  • Processing geo-spatial data and data coming from the 'Internet of things' devices (e.g. iBeacons, MQTT)
  • Data correlation with external streams (social, weather, exchange data, etc)

Requirements

  • BS or MS degree (or ongoing) in Computer Science or equivalent experience in the industry
  • Advanced Java programmer skills with a good grasp of key computer science fundamentals including algorithms, data structures, multi-threading.
  • Linux/Unix experience and understanding of LXC/Docker is desirable
  • Strong oral and written English communication skills
  • Experience or interest as Open Source contributor desirable
  • Hadoop 2.0/YARN experience is desirable but not exclusive

Last but not the least, candidates should be excited to help build our YARN/Hadoop 2.0 based ecosystem and to participate in building our company culture as expressed by our core values. If you are in search of an opportunity for a startup company that fosters innovation and creativity, while delivering cutting edge technologies, then submit your resume today.
